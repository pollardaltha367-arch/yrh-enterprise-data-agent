# Business Analysis Skill

## Purpose

Convert data into business decisions instead of just charts.

## Input

- Clean data
- Business question
- Metric definitions

## Process

1. Define core metrics.
2. Calculate overall KPIs.
3. Break metrics by time, category, channel, user, and inventory.
4. Find top contributors and risk areas.
5. Generate business actions.

## Output

- KPI table
- Analysis tables
- Chart list
- Key findings
- Business recommendations

## Quality Standard

Recommendations must connect to data. Avoid empty advice such as "improve operations" without evidence.
